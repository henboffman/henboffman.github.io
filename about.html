How Website Arena Works

Website Arena fetches two websites, extracts their HTML elements, converts them into battle units, and simulates a turn-based combat between the two armies. Here's how every system works.

Stat Calculations

After base stats are assigned, content-based bonuses are applied:

ConditionBonus
Text content length > 0+HP (0.3 per char, max +20)
Child elements > 0+DEF (1.5 per child, max +15)
Attributes > 2+ATK (1 per attr, max +8)
Has CSS classes+5 HP, +3 DEF

Per-Element Accessibility Bonuses

Individual elements can earn stat bonuses from their own accessibility features:

FeatureBonus

Has aria-label or aria-labelledby+8 HP
Has meaningful role attribute+4 DEF
<input> with associated <label>+5 DEF

Combat Mechanics

Damage Formula

damage = max(1, round((attacker.ATK * lastStandMult - defender.DEF + random(-3, 5)) * critMult))

Where lastStandMult is 1.4 if the attacker is below 15% HP, and critMult is 2.5 on a critical hit (1.0 otherwise).

Critical Hit Chance

critChance = max(0, 0.10 + streak * 0.05 + (level - 1) * 0.05 - defender.critResist)

Base 10%, increased by win streaks and level, reduced by the defender's a11y crit resistance.

Dodge

Only available to armies with the A11y Champion tier (8% chance). A dodged attack deals 0 damage.

Counter Attack

After the main exchange, Striker and Vanguard roles have a 15% chance to counter for 50% of their ATK. If the counter defeats the target, it counts as a kill.

Last Stand

When a unit drops below 15% HP, it enters Last Stand mode: a 1.4x damage multiplier on all attacks. Visually marked with a red pulsing glow.

Healing

Each round has a 25% chance of triggering a healing phase. A random Engineer heals a random wounded ally for round(engineer.DEF * 0.5 + random(3, 8)) HP, up to max HP.

Morale

When a Boss or Commander is defeated, the entire army suffers a morale break:

  • Boss death: −12% ATK and DEF for all surviving units
  • Commander death: −6% ATK and DEF for all surviving units

XP and Leveling

Units earn XP by defeating enemies:

xpGain = 10 + round((victim.maxHP + victim.ATK) * 0.15)

XP needed to level up: 30 * currentLevel. Maximum level is 5.

On level up:

  • +4 ATK
  • +3 DEF
  • +15% of max HP (added to both current and max)

Battle Flow

  1. Both armies are shuffled randomly
  2. Each round, a variable number of pairs fight simultaneously (1–12, based on intensity roll)
  3. Higher ATK+HP units are weighted to fight more often
  4. After all duels in a round, 25% chance of a healing phase
  5. HP bars update, then the next round begins
  6. Battle ends when one army has no surviving units
