FigureLinda Moulton-Howe

Investigative journalist; Emmy Award winner; author of *A Strange Harvest* (1980) and *An Alien Harvest* (1989). Long association with the cattle-mutilation and UAP reporting community.

What's documented

Investigative journalist and documentary filmmaker. Won an Emmy Award for A Strange Harvest (1980), the documentary that brought cattle mutilations into national public attention. Author of An Alien Harvest (1989), one of the field’s foundational long-form cattle-mutilation reference works.

Has maintained a multi-decade reporting practice on UAP, cattle mutilation, and adjacent anomalous phenomena. Appeared on The Secret of Skinwalker Ranch S1E7 (‘Surveillance’) as a guest investigator brought in to assess the season’s cattle-mutilation incident against her existing case files. Her appearance ties Skinwalker Ranch’s cattle-mutilation phenomena into the broader phenomenon-cattle-mutilations entry in this corpus.
