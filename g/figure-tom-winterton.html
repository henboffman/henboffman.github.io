What's documented

Ranch superintendent at Skinwalker Ranch under Fugal’s ownership. Multi-generational Uintah Basin local; functions on-screen as both the day-to-day operational manager and as the show’s most consistent eyewitness reservoir — Winterton is on-site when the team is not, and he carries the residual reports between filming windows.

His S1E3 headache incident (during the Homestead 2 basement bone investigation) is one of the season’s most-cited ‘personnel illness’ data points and established the show’s recurring ‘the ranch reacts to investigation’ trope.
