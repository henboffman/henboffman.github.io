I'm Not Like You

The documented record of unidentified aerial phenomena, mapped as one graph.

Three ways in

  1. Open the constellation Every place, figure, case, and phenomenon as one graph. Drag to pan, scroll to zoom, click any node to enter the underlying material. Filter by type. The shape of the discourse, visible.
  2. Browse the index Fifty-five real places (Roswell, Rendlesham, Kola, Vostok, Dyatlov Pass…), ninety-plus figures and cases and ongoing phenomena, organized by category. Photo grid + bucketed lists.
  3. The creepy index Hand-curated rank-order of the items in the public record that don't get easier with re-reading. Three windows: historical (pre-1990), 1990-2015, post-2015. Same sourcing standard across all three.

Every entry is documented public record. Named witnesses, court cases, declassified memos, FOIA returns, peer-reviewed papers, federal accident reports. Working draft. The graph keeps growing.
