What's documented

Marfa is a small city in Presidio County, Texas, at the intersection of US 90 and US 67 in the high desert of the Trans-Pecos region. Founded as a railroad water stop in the 1880s. Population ~1,800. The "Marfa lights," recurring luminous phenomena reported from a viewing area east of town since the late 19th century, are an established item of West Texas folklore; their physical mechanism has not been definitively determined by mainstream investigation.
