Wright-Patterson AFB

39°49′N, 84°02′W — Greene/Montgomery County, Ohio — United States Air Force base, headquarters of Air Force Materiel Command

What's documented

Wright-Patterson Air Force Base is a United States Air Force installation in Ohio, formed in 1948 by merging Wright Field and Patterson Field. It is the headquarters of Air Force Materiel Command and one of the largest USAF bases. Foreign Technology Division (later National Air and Space Intelligence Center) and Project Blue Book ran out of Wright-Patterson; the base has been a persistent fixture in U.S. aviation, intelligence, and (in popular lore) UAP/UFO history since the late 1940s.
